SSTV From The ISS

At the very end of last year, the ISS started transmitting SSTV signals. The pictures sent were part of a 12 image series detailing the exploration of the Moon.

While I have dabbled in local SSTV (I'm lucky enough to live inside recieve range of and VHF SSTV repeater), decoding signals from the ISS was a particular thrill.
